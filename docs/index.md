## About This Handbook

The Event Management Handbook is a comprehensive guide designed for IT professionals, service management practitioners, and organizations implementing or optimizing ITIL/ITSM Event Management processes.

### What You'll Learn

This handbook covers everything you need to know about Event Management:

- **Foundations** - Core concepts, strategic framework, and critical success factors
- **Framework** - Event classification, prioritization, roles, and process activities
- **Technical Implementation** - Monitoring, correlation, automation, and ITSM integration
- **Metrics & Improvement** - KPIs, reporting, dashboards, and maturity models
- **Governance** - Control objectives, policies, standards, and compliance
- **Implementation** - Roadmaps, best practices, common pitfalls, and practical guidance

### Who Should Read This

- **IT Operations Managers** - Learn how to implement and optimize Event Management
- **Service Managers** - Understand integration with Incident, Problem, and Change Management
- **Technical Architects** - Design effective monitoring and automation solutions
- **ITIL/ITSM Practitioners** - Deepen your Event Management knowledge
- **DevOps Teams** - Bridge traditional ITSM with modern operational practices
- **Students & Educators** - Comprehensive curriculum for training and certification

### Key Features

- ✅ **19 Comprehensive Chapters** organized into 6 logical parts
- ✅ **8 Critical Success Factors** for implementation success
- ✅ **6 Key Performance Indicators** with targets and formulas
- ✅ **5 Maturity Levels** to guide your progression
- ✅ **8 Control Objectives** for governance and compliance
- ✅ **Real-World Examples** and practical guidance throughout

### Book Structure

#### Part I: Foundations (Chapters 1-3)

#### Part II: Framework (Chapters 4-7)

Roadmap, best practices, and moving forward

### How to Use This Handbook

**For Beginners:** Start with Part I to understand foundational concepts, then progress through each part sequentially.

**For Experienced Practitioners:** Use the sidebar navigation to jump directly to specific chapters based on your needs.

**For Implementers:** Focus on Parts III, V, and VI for practical implementation guidance.

**For Managers:** Review Parts I, IV, and VI for strategic insights and business value.
